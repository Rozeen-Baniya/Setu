FAQs About Sign Language

Is Nepali Sign Language the same as gestures?

No. NSL is a structured language with its own grammar and vocabulary, while gestures are just casual movements.

Can Setu help me learn Nepali Sign Language?

Yes! By using Setu, you'll naturally pick up signs and improve your understanding over time.

Who can benefit from Setu?

Students, teachers, families, and anyone who wants to bridge the communication gap with the deaf community.

Is there a difference between deaf and hearing impaired people?

From a clinical point of view, what differentiates the deaf from the hearing impaired is the depth of the hearing loss.
